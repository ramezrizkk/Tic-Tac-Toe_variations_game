Detailed Description

Ultimate Tic-Tac-Toe on a 9x9 grid composed of nine 3x3 zones.

Provides SuperGrid and MegaGameInterface classes. Players play on a local 3x3 zone, and winning a zone marks it on the global 3x3 grid. Win the global grid to win the game.
