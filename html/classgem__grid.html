Detailed Description

7x7 diamond-shaped board for Diamond Tic-Tac-Toe.

Out-of-bounds cells are marked with space. Win requires both a triplet (3 marks) and quartet (4 marks) in different orientations (horizontal, vertical, diagonal, anti-diagonal).
